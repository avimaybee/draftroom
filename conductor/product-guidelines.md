# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Voice**: Conversational and supportive. The system should act as a reliable partner or assistant rather than a cold utility.
- **Tone**: Encouraging yet professional. Use "we" and "I" to foster a collaborative feel between the agent and the human operator.
- **Precision**: While conversational, descriptions must remain accurate, especially when presenting research findings.

## Visual Identity & Branding
- **Aesthetic**: Modern Agency Premium. The UI should reflect the high-end creative nature of the agency itself—polished, clean, and visually sophisticated.
- **Feedback**: Use subtle animations and high-quality typography to convey a "premium" experience.
- **Clarity**: Despite the premium feel, information density should be managed to prevent overwhelm.

## User Experience Principles
- **Autonomous Execution**: The system is designed for "fire and forget" background tasks. The agent performs comprehensive research independently.
- **Review & Feedback Loop**: After background work is complete, the human operator reviews findings. This "Checkpoint" is crucial for steering the agent's next steps (e.g., qualifying leads, ignoring certain sectors).
- **Human-Centric Control**: The human always has the final word on high-stakes actions like outreach or lead qualification.

## AI Trust & Transparency
- **Explainable Logic**: Every suggestion or research finding should come with a "Why this?" explanation or a summary of the reasoning process.
- **Source Attribution**: Where possible, link back to original sources (LinkedIn profiles, websites, etc.) to build trust in the agent's research.
